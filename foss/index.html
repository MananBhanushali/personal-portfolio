World Animal Day

In 2017 I designed a series of posters on the occasion of World Animal Day. Each animal is made up of the same primary shapes and stares curiously at us. For once they look at us like we look at them in the zoo: what is the human doing? They seem to challenge us to think about our own behaviour.
